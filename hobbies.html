My Hobbies

Soccer

Softball

I have played soccer almost my entire life. I played on AYSO until I was 8, and then started to play club for Chicago Northwind. I always wanted to be the kid that punted the ball, so I started playing gaolkeeper. I played Northwind for 6 years before my team started to fall apart. We went 1-16-7 my last season with Northwind. I became really close with all my teamates, but I needed to make something better for myself. I decided to move to a club way up north call FC United. I just finished my first season with them, and I made the right choice moving clubs. We finished in a very tough Elite 64 league 4-4, and made it to the state cup.
I started playing softball in 5th grade at Hamlin Park. I played catch with my dad in the backyard all the time, but I want to start playing seriously. After my first season with Hamlin House league, I decided to start playing travel there. I developed as a third baseman and a catcher. My favorite feeling was throwing out people that were trying to steal. I played travel for 2 years, and then before high school started, I quit so I could play soccer. At Lane Tech, I have a much better chance of making the soccer team rather than the softball team.
